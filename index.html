Important Drainage Design Notes:

• Drainage lines should maintain self-cleansing velocity.
• Flat slopes may cause blockage.
• Excessive slope may separate solids and liquid flow.
• Manholes normally required at:
- Change in direction
- Change in pipe size
- Change in gradient
- Maximum manhole to manhole spacing considered in this calculator is 15m as per your required DM/UAE practice.

This calculator currently follows maximum 15m spacing checking for drainage design practice.

Professional Engineering Guidance

Commonly Followed Standards in UAE:
• Dubai Municipality Drainage & Irrigation Department Standards
• Dubai Building Code (DBC)
• Local Order No. 8 of 2002 regarding Sewage, Irrigation and Water Drainage
• Trakhees Drainage Regulation DD-9.0
• BS EN 12056 Gravity Drainage Systems
• BS EN 752 Drain and Sewer Systems
• BS EN 1610 Sewer Construction and Testing


Recommended Design Practice:
✔ Maintain minimum self-cleansing velocity around 0.75 m/s.
✔ Avoid too flat slope.
✔ Avoid too steep slope causing solids separation.
✔ Use inspection chambers/manholes properly.
✔ Always coordinate with authority approved drainage drawings.
